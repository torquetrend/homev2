Genesis Electrified G80 Review

By Emily Davis | Published on October 28, 2024

The Genesis Electrified G80 marks a significant step for the luxury brand as it ventures into the electric vehicle market. Combining elegant design, cutting-edge technology, and impressive performance, the Electrified G80 aims to compete with established players in the electric luxury sedan segment.

Design and Exterior

The Electrified G80 maintains the sophisticated design language of its gasoline counterpart, with subtle tweaks to distinguish it as an electric model. The signature Crest Grille is now a sleek, aerodynamic front fascia, and the charging port is cleverly integrated into the grille for a seamless look.

Key Exterior Features

  • LED quad headlights and taillights
  • 19-inch aerodynamic alloy wheels
  • Panoramic sunroof

Interior and Comfort

The interior of the Electrified G80 exudes luxury and comfort. High-quality materials, including Nappa leather and open-pore wood trim, create an upscale ambiance. The cabin is spacious, with ample legroom and headroom for all passengers. The absence of engine noise enhances the serene environment.

Technology Highlights

  • 14.5-inch touchscreen infotainment system
  • 12.3-inch 3D digital instrument cluster
  • Lexicon premium audio system with 21 speakers

Performance and Range

Powered by dual electric motors, the Electrified G80 delivers a combined output of 365 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. The all-wheel-drive system ensures excellent traction and handling. The vehicle offers an estimated range of up to 282 miles on a single charge, making it suitable for both daily commutes and longer trips.

Charging and Battery

The Electrified G80 supports fast charging, allowing it to charge from 10% to 80% in approximately 22 minutes using a 350 kW DC fast charger. For home charging, it takes around 7 hours to fully charge using a Level 2 charger.

“Genesis has seamlessly integrated electric technology into their luxury sedan, setting a new standard in the segment.” — Automotive Analyst

Safety Features

Safety is a priority in the Electrified G80, which comes equipped with a comprehensive suite of driver-assistance technologies, including:

  • Smart Cruise Control with Stop & Go
  • Highway Driving Assist II
  • Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Blind-Spot Collision-Avoidance Assist

Verdict

The Genesis Electrified G80 successfully combines luxury, performance, and electric efficiency. It stands out in the crowded electric sedan market with its distinctive design and value proposition. For those seeking an upscale electric vehicle without compromising on style or comfort, the Electrified G80 is a compelling choice.

Pros

  • Elegant and sophisticated design
  • Luxurious and spacious interior
  • Competitive electric range and fast charging capabilities
  • Advanced technology and safety features

Cons

  • Limited availability in some markets
  • Brand recognition still growing compared to established luxury competitors
